What to do

Trust the credential, not the paper. Cross-reference the figures on the credential page with the paper in your hand. If they differ, the paper has been edited after the supplier signed.

Likely scenarios: (1) honest copy-paste error when the cert was reprinted, (2) deliberate alteration of a property to make a marginal heat appear in-spec. Either way — escalate to supplier quality and do not release the material.
